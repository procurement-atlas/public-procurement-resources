# Public procurement in Europe: a curated resource list

A curated, maintained list of useful resources: official portals, directives, tools and specialist sites worth reading. Updated 2026-07-13. Contributions welcome via pull request.


## Public procurement and tendering

### Official sources

- [TED - Tenders Electronic Daily](https://ted.europa.eu/) - the official EU tender database, all above-threshold notices
- [SIMAP](https://simap.ted.europa.eu/) - EU procurement forms, CPV codes and thresholds
- [EU public procurement directives](https://single-market-economy.ec.europa.eu/single-market/public-procurement_en) - Directive 2014/24/EU and guidance
- [Upphandlingsmyndigheten](https://www.upphandlingsmyndigheten.se/) - the Swedish National Agency for Public Procurement
- [TenderNed](https://www.tenderned.nl/) - the Dutch national tender portal
- [Open Contracting Partnership](https://www.open-contracting.org/) - open data standards for public contracting
- [OECD Public Procurement](https://www.oecd.org/en/topics/public-procurement.html) - international benchmarks and integrity guidance

### Specialist sites and guides

- [tenderplaybook.com](https://www.tenderplaybook.com/) - Tenderplaybook.com är kunskapsnavet i procurement-klustret, en flerspråkig handbok om offentlig upphandling och anbud.
- [publicwins.com](https://www.publicwins.com/) - Publicwins.com är konverteringsnoden i procurement-klustret.
- [offervo.com](https://www.offervo.com/) - Offervo.com är verktygs- och produktnoden i procurement-klustret.


## Legal frameworks

### Official sources

- [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/) - EU law in all official languages
- [WTO Agreement on Government Procurement](https://www.wto.org/english/tratop_e/gproc_e/gp_gpa_e.htm) - the international GPA framework
